📜 Legal & Compliance

Terms, Conditions & Compliance

📋 Terms of Use

  • All users must be 18+ and legally authorised to trade waste in their jurisdiction.
  • Listings must accurately describe waste type, quantity, condition and location.
  • Prohibited: hazardous radioactive waste, illegal dumping, misrepresentation of goods.
  • Platform fees: 2.5% transaction fee, free listings for first 90 days.
  • Disputes resolved within 48 hours via our mediation team.

🔒 Privacy & Data

  • NDPR (Nigeria), GDPR (EU) and POPIA (South Africa) compliant data processing.
  • KYC data encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 standard.
  • We never sell personal data to third parties without explicit consent.
  • Data retained only as long as necessary for legal and operational purposes.
  • Users may request data deletion at any time from account settings.

🌍 International Trade Compliance

  • Waste exports comply with Basel Convention on Hazardous Wastes.
  • Users are responsible for obtaining necessary export and import permits.
  • Platform provides document templates — not legal advice.
  • All cross-border transactions subject to applicable customs duties.

🤝 Escrow & Payments

  • Funds held in escrow until buyer confirms delivery or 5 business days pass.
  • Refunds issued within 3 business days for verified failed transactions.
  • Platform charges no FX fees — live interbank rates applied.
  • Tax responsibilities remain with the buyer/seller per local laws.
